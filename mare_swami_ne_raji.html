Mare Swami Ne Raji


Māre Swāmīne rājī karvā ja chhe I want to make Swami happy
Māre Shrījīne rājī karvā ja chhe I want to make Shreeji happy
Koī paṇ bhoge game te sanjoge In any situation and action
Swāmīne rājī karvā ja chhe I want to make swami happy

Vahelā ūṭhī Prabhusmaraṇ karīne wake up early and remember god
Snān pūjā karī mātā pitāne Take a shower, do puja, and bow down...
Praṇām māre karvā ja chhe ...I want to bow down to my parents
